Hi, I'm Daniel and this is my cocktail website. I am novice in mixology but I think it's a great deal of fun and I would like to share it with people. Below are all the recipes I have tried and check often for more.

Breakfast Martini

  • 40ml London Dry Gin
  • 20ml triple sec
  • 10ml lemon juice
  • 1 barspoon of marmalade
  • Shaken

Lots of my recent cocktail have been very citrusy and here's another to add to that theme. This cocktail (and the last) is great if you have some orange marmalade lying around, it adds sweetness and a unique flavour to an otherwise standard cocktail.29/03/2020

Paddington Bear

  • 50ml London Dry Gin
  • 25ml Campari
  • 37.5ml orange juice
  • 37.5ml lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons of marmalade
  • dash of Angostura orange bitters
  • Lightly shaken

A local recipe made with local gin from Archangel Distilleries. It has a good balance of bitterness from the Campari and the sweetness from the orange components. It's really good to get another recipe to use up the Campari, since I don't use it that much.27/03/20

Honeysuckle Daiquiri

  • 60ml White Rum
  • 22.5ml lemon juice
  • 22.5 honey syrup (2:1)
  • Shaken

A twist on a classic daiquiri. Has a subtle honey note that I really like and it is another recipe that will help me use up my honey syrup.03/03/2020

Midori Sour

  • 60ml Midori Liqueur
  • 30ml lime juice
  • 3/4 egg white
  • Dry then wet shaken

Has a wonderful melon taste and a nice foam. Midori seems to make a good base by itself as no other spirit is used in this drink. It's a great one to make for other people as it has an impressive colour. It's a brilliant showstopper!03/03/2020

Gin Fizz

  • 60ml London Dry Gin
  • 22.5ml lemon juice
  • 22.5ml simple syrup
  • 1/2 egg white
  • top with soda water
  • Dry then wet shaken

This recipe has very simple ingredients and is very easy to make. It is a great introduction to sours because it is lengthened by the soda water, and the combination of fizz and egg white can lead to some impressive foaming if done right. Of course this was my first try so it didn't quite stand up on it's own in this photo.08/02/20

Espresso Martini

  • 60ml Vodka
  • 50ml Kahlúa liqueur
  • 50ml espresso
  • Shaken

An iconic recipe that seems to be on the menu at every bar and club I've ever walked into. If you want to be fancy then get the espresso fresh from a coffee machine, though if you are like me and don't have one then use 36g of fine ground coffee in a french press. I even managed to get instant coffee working at a friends house but I wouldn't recommend it.04/12/19
